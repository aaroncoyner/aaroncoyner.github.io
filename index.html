Meet
Aaron Coyner

Aaron is a second-year Bioinformatics and Computational Biology PhD Student at Oregon Health & Science University (OHSU) and a National Library of Medicine (NLM) research fellow. He works closely with the i-ROP Consortium studying retinopathy of prematurity, and his dissertation work centers around the implementation of machine learning algorithms for retinal fundus image quality assessment and abnormal pathological findings.

When Aaron isn't studying, coding, or writing papers, he can usually be found grabbing beers with his girlfriend, Jessica, hiking or playing in the park with his dog, Zoey, or exploring the Pacific Northwest on his Triumph Tiger - which doesn't have a name...

...yet 😋
